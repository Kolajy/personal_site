Apple Mail AI Classifier connects natively to macOS Apple Mail via AppleScript and uses a local Ollama model (Qwen 3.5 4B) to read, categorize, and clean up your inbox. It operates entirely on-device, offering smart automated actions (archive, junk, flag, or folder routing) with zero cloud dependency.
